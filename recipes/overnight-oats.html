Overnight Oats

Overnight Oats Base

While technically you only need rolled oats and milk to make overnight oats, I always add chia seeds, yogurt, and a smidge of maple syrup in my base recipe. Thatâ€™s what makes them ultra creamy and tasty!

Ingredients

  • rolled oats
  • milk
  • yogurt
  • chia seeds
  • honey or maple syrup

Preparation

  1. Add all of the ingredients into a sealable container and mix well
  2. Let it soak in fridge overnight
  3. Top your oats with your favorite toppings and enjoy!
