Frozen Hot Chocolate

What is Frozen Hot Chocolate?

There are two very important things to remember when it comes to serving frozen hot chocolate: Drink it as soon as you blend it (although it still tastes pretty good melted) and do not forget to top it off with a generous serving of homemade whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups ice
  • 2 cups chocolate milk
  • 1 cup powdered hot chocolate mix
  • whipped cream (optional)
  • chocolate shavings or sprinkles

Preparation

  1. Place 4 cups ice, 2 cups chocolate milk, 1 cup powdered hot chocolate mix in a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until completely smooth, about 1 minute
  3. Divide the frozen hot chocolate into 4 glasses. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings or sprinkles, if desired.
